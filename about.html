About Us.

We are furry friends. A dog daycare, training and grooming service based in county Wicklow.
Our services that we offer are both top quality and affordable for you.
Whether you need need someone to look after your dog during the day while your in work or going on holidays for a couple of days we have you covered. Whether your dog is not smelling the best and you want them Professionally washed and cleaned we have you covered. If you have a hyper little puppy or any dog of any age and want to calm them down or make them more behaved or teach them some tricks we have you covered. Its why we have been open for 25 years.
